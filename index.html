My Favourite Place to Visit

My favourite place to visit is Tinkertown because I really like the fun rides there. My favourites rides there is the boat that goes really high up and then comes back down and the ride that spins in circles really fast. I also liked the ferris wheel there but the ferris wheel was slow but really high up. There was another ferris wheel and that ferris wheel was tilted, so it was going really fast and a little scary. Tinkertown was really crowded because there was no Covid-19 back then and it was hard looking for a parking spot. I've only gone to Tinkertown once and it's really fun to go there. You need to buy a ticket first so you can ride the rides there. Tinkertown is really good and I enjoyed my time there.
